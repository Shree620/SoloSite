My Major and Desired Career Path

As a Pennsylvania State Undergraduate Student, I am currently a Data Science major in the
college of Informational Technology Systems. Data Science is a passion that I have discovered while I enrolled in my first Data Science
Course at my university. In this course, I learned several languages:

    Python-Pandas, PostgreSQl, MongoDB, Neo4J

I have taken advantage of these languages in order to be able to create complex queries which extract data from a database and CSV files and to clean up the extracted data.
I have also developed data analysis skills so I can convey my extracted data in an easy to read format.

I hope to continue improving my data science skills so I can choose a career path as a data analyst.



My major requires that I spend some time participating in research or an internship in order to graduate.
I also need to take a variety of data science courses that will help me develop my skills even further.
Some of these include:

    Artificial Intelligence, Programming Models for Big Data, Network Analytics

Career Path

I am committed into going into the career path for a data analyst. From the words of the Pennsylvania
State College of IST,



"The main duties of a Data Analyst are to perform data collection, analysis,
interpretation, and management duties; develop rules and methodologies for data collection and analysis;
and ensure the integrity of project data. Data analysts consult with external clients and internal staff
to determine data management needs and objectives, and may be responsible for database design,
administration, security, and maintenance. They prepare presentations that reflect the results of data
analysis, and coordinate with scientists and technical experts to design and develop statistical
analysis methodologies."
